Johnson & Johnson Vaccine

Criteria To Take The Johnson & Johnson Vaccine :


The Following Can Be Vaccinated :

Persons 18 years and over who are advised to take the vaccine

Persons living with HIV who are recommended for vaccination

Immunocompromised persons who are recommended for vaccination

Persons with autoimmune disorders who are recommended for vaccination


The Following Can't Be Vaccinated :

Persons below the age of 18

Persons who are pregnant

Persons who have had an allergic reaction to the vaccine or a component of the vaccine

Persons who are COVID-19 positive

If you are a fan of The Dark Tower books by Stephen King, you many not enjoy this screen adaptation as it tries to condense the entire series into 94 minuites. The plot of the movie revolves around stopping the main antagonist known as "The Man In Black," from destroying the Dark Tower. To stop this from happening the main protagonist Jack Chambers must use his psychic visions as a guide to find the last Gunslinger. The two then face off against "The Man In Black" in a fight to preserve the balance of all worlds.
