Sinopharm Vaccine

Criteria To Take The Sinopharm Vaccine :


The Following Can Be Vaccinated :

Persons 18 years and over who are advised to take the vaccine

Persons living with HIV who are recommended for vaccination

Immunocompromised persons who are recommended for vaccination

Persons with autoimmune disorders who are recommended for vaccination


The Following Can't Be Vaccinated :

Persons below the age of 18

Persons who are pregnant

Persons who have had an allergic reaction to the vaccine or a component of the vaccine

Persons who are COVID-19 positive

Sinopharm Vaccine Information :


The Sinopharm vaccine was developed by the state-owned China National Pharmaceutical Group (Sinopharm) and the Beijing Institute of Biological Products (BIBP) using the means of an inactivated virus. The coronavirus which causes COVID-19 was modified in such a way that it is unable to replicate, making this vaccine suitable for those with a compromised immune system.


This vaccine is recommended for use in the form of two doses with a three-to four-week interval between doses. It should also be administered within at least a 14-day interval after any other vaccines.
